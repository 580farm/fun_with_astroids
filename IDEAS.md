# Ideas

This is the parking lot for good ideas that should not distract from the next
small playable milestone.

## Game Modes

- Story path through spaceflight eras.
- Endless asteroid field.
- Time trial levels.
- Local co-op with two spacecraft.
- Museum quiz mode.
- Daily challenge seed.

## Level Ideas

- Early Orbit: slow asteroids, wide spacing.
- Lunar Transit: fewer pickups, longer timer.
- Shuttle Corridor: larger ship, tighter gaps.
- Station Approach: avoid debris around a docking lane.
- Deep Space: low visibility, radar becomes valuable.

## Powerups

- Temporary shield.
- Short boost burst.
- Radar ping.
- Slow-motion pulse.
- Repair kit.
- Credit token.
- Trajectory preview.

## Hazards

- Tumbling asteroids.
- Fast micrometeoroids.
- Debris clusters.
- Gravity wells.
- Sensor blackout zones.
- Narrow docking lanes.

## Museum Features

- Spacecraft gallery.
- Timeline of first flights.
- Country or program filters.
- One-paragraph mission summaries.
- Kid-friendly vocabulary notes.

## Accessibility Ideas

- Colorblind-safe palettes.
- Reduced motion setting.
- Adjustable game speed.
- Larger HUD text.
- No-flashing mode.
- Remappable controls.

## Nice-to-Haves

- Controller support.
- Replay ghost.
- Family high-score initials.
- Unlockable concept art.
- Simple level editor.
- Packaged builds for macOS, Windows, and Linux.
